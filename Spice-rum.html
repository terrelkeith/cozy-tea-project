Spice Rum

Total Time: 30 min

Servings: 8-10 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 cups almond milk
  • 5 spiced chai tea bags
  • 8 ounces spiced rum or bourbon
  • 3 ounces agave syrup
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • Ground cinnamon, for garnish

Directions

  • 1. Bring 4 cups water and the almond milk to a boil. Remove from the heat and add the tea bags. Let steep for 10 minutes. While the mixture is still warm, stir in the spiced rum and agave.

  • 2. While the tea is steeping, whip the cream to soft peaks.

  • 3. Remove the tea bags and ladle the spiked chai into serving cups. Top with a dollop of whipped cream and a dusting of ground cinnamon.
